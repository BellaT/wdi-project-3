Some recommended products for helping your survival chances.

Guns

Lock 'n load up on all the guns you can get your hands on. They will probably come in handy.

Swords

If guns aren't your thing, why not try a top of the range Excalibur replica sword?

Goggles

Zombie slaying can be pretty messy, so to prevent those of brain or chunks of guts getting in your eyes, make sure you pick up some protective eye wear.

Survival Kits

If you've never been a particularly outdoorsy person, you may not have your own survival kit? Well look no further!

Water

Staying hydrated is essential, especially when you're constantly running away from zombies. So if you're lucky, there's a pop-up shop near you that is dedicated to H2O.

Night Vision

Watch the action all day round. Zombies aren't so great at night so you could catch them off guard and slay them with ease using a pair of night vision goggles.
